Introduction

Welcome to the Technical Doc Project made on freeCodeCamp!

You will see in this page how elements interact with others in the game genshin impact!

The game have 7 elements, but we will not talk much about the Dendro one...

Note: The elemental reaction is to see as: second is the element already on the target, first is your elemental attack

Here are the list os elements:

  • Anemo
  • Geo
  • Pyro
  • Electro
  • Cryo
  • Hydro
  • Dendro
Anemo!

Anemo is the name for wind element, witch is my favorite of the game.

The reaction of the anemo is swirl, a reaction whose take one element in contact and spread it effect causing other reactions!

Here are Anemo combinations:

Anemo + Pyro
Anemo + Hydro
Anemo + Cryo
Anemo + Electro
Geo!

The Geo element is the earth element.

The interaction is similar to the anemo, but it consume de element and create a crystal shard wich creates a elemental shield for who picks. The reaction is called crystallize

Here are Geo combinations:

Geo + Pyro = Pyro Shield
Geo + Hydro = Hydro Shield
Geo + Cryo = Cryo Shield
Geo + Electro = Electro Shield
Pyro!

Pyro is the fire element

Unlike Anemo and Geo, Pyro have diferent reaction with each elements.

Here are Pyro combinations:

Pyro + Cryo = Melt, deals 2x Pyro dmg.
Pyro + Hydro = Vaporize, (reversed) deals 1.5x Pyro dmg.
Pyro + Electro = Overload, deals a explosion with AoE Pyro dmg
Pyro + Dendro = Burning, deals continuous Pyro dmg.
Electro!

It's easy associate Electro with eletricity.

Like Pyro, it have differenct reactions:

Electro + Pyro = Overload, deals a explosion with AoE Pyro dmg
Electro + Hydro = Eletro-charge, deals continuous Electro dmg.
Electro + Cryo = Superconduct, deals AoE cryo dmg and reduces target physical defense.
Cryo!

Cryo is the element that represent ice.

Enemys affected by cryo moves slow.

Here are the combinations:

Cryo + Pyro = Melt (reversed) deals 1.5x cryo dmg
Cryo + Hydro = Frozen, freezes enemy in place.
Cryo + Electro = Superconduct, deals AoE cryo dmg and reduces target physical defense.
Hydro!

Hydro it's the water element.

It effects are similar to the other.

The combinations are all listed above, here a resume:

Hydro + Cryo = Frozen, freezes enemy in place.
Hydro + Electro = Eletro-charge, deals continuous Electro dmg.
Hydro + Pyro = Vaporize, deals 2x Hydro dmg.
Dendro!

Dendro is the representative of nature.

It's a reworked element in the game, the reactions are all new and i will not list them because of this.
